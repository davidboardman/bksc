We are Brooklyn Synchro Club



Bringing synchronized swimming to the people and pools of Brooklyn and the boroughs of NYC. We're a non-exclusive, coed club where members learn, experiment, and challenge themselves in a fun environment.

Themed events and speciality workouts with like-minded mermaids.
